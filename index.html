FEATURED RESEARCH

Q-TriM

ECCV 2026

Q-TriM: Question-Guided Tri-Modal Attention for Audio-Visual Question Answering

European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV 2026)

Q-TriM is a shallow and parallel multimodal attention framework for Audio-Visual Question Answering. It captures question-guided interactions among video, audio, and text while reducing unnecessary sequential fusion.

  • Question-guided tri-modal attention design
  • Shallow and parallel multimodal fusion
  • Token filtering with straight-through estimation
  • End-to-end implementation, experiments, and paper writing
